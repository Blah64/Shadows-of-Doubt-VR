SoDVR — VR Controller Mapping
==============================

RIGHT CONTROLLER
----------------
Trigger              UI click (point at menu and pull)
A button             Jump (Space)
B button             Notebook / Map (Tab)
Grip                 Drag canvas panels (CaseBoard, menus)
Thumbstick left/right  Snap turn ±30°
Thumbstick up/down   Scroll VR Settings panel (when open)
Thumbstick click     Flashlight toggle

LEFT CONTROLLER
---------------
Y / Menu button      Pause menu (ESC)
X button             Crouch toggle (C)
Trigger              Interact — doors, objects, NPCs (E)
Grip                 Inventory (X)
Thumbstick           Move player (head-relative)
Thumbstick click     Sprint toggle (auto-stops when stick returns to centre)

NOTES
-----
- Sprint (left thumbstick click) holds Shift while active.
  It stops automatically when you stop moving the left stick.
- Right trigger fires UI clicks when pointing at a menu canvas,
  and world-interact (E) when nothing is in view — use left
  trigger for reliable world interaction.
- VR Settings panel: press F10 or the in-game Settings button.
  Right stick scrolls the panel while it is open.
- Snap turn angle and other comfort settings are in the VR
  Settings panel.
