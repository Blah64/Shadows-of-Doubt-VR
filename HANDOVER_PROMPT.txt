SHORT SUMMARY:

We are switching from a VDXR-internal machine-code patching approach to standard OpenXR loader P/Invoke (the same approach used by LCVR, CWVR, UnityVRMod). The old approach (git 1be2b0e) got swapchain creation working but produced null texture pointers because VDXR's internal streaming pool (robj+0x990) was never populated despite bypassing 14 machine-code guards.
